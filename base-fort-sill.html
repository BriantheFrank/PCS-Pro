Army Duty Station

Fort Sill, Oklahoma

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Sill is a historic Army installation in southwestern Oklahoma that supports field artillery and air defense training, education, and operational readiness.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers report to the Welcome Center or Soldier Support Center for in-processing and administrative intake.

Commissary

The Fort Sill commissary provides DeCA grocery services on post.

Fort Sill Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange facilities provide retail and service options on the installation.

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • U.S. Army Field Artillery School
  • U.S. Army Air Defense Artillery School
  • 434th Field Artillery Brigade
  • 75th Field Artillery Brigade
