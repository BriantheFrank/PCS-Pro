Army Duty Station

Fort Riley, Kansas

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Riley is a major Army installation in northeastern Kansas that supports mechanized infantry, sustainment, and training missions for the 1st Infantry Division.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers report to the Soldier Support Center or Reception Company for in-processing and administrative intake.

Commissary

The Fort Riley commissary provides DeCA grocery services on post.

PX / Exchange

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • 1st Infantry Division
  • 1st Sustainment Brigade
  • Combat Aviation Brigade, 1st Infantry Division
  • 1st Infantry Division Artillery
