U.S. Military PCS Toolkit

PCS Move Checklist

A practical, offline-friendly checklist to guide each phase of your Permanent Change of Station move.

Orders Received

Confirm report dates, gaining unit, and any special instructions so you can plan timelines correctly.

Ask your chain of command if your date shifted or requires official amendments.

Share the move timeline with spouses, caregivers, and anyone helping with dependents.

Pre-Move Planning

Track expected travel costs, temporary lodging, and out-of-pocket expenses to reduce surprises.

Compare on-post and off-post options, waitlists, and neighborhood commute times.

Store copies of vaccinations, school transcripts, and vet documentation for easy check-in.

Scheduling Movers

Choose a government-arranged move or personally procured move and document the decision.

Coordinate with the transportation office to lock in packing and pickup windows.

Verify authorized weight limits to avoid excess weight charges and plan accordingly.

Packing Period

Keep uniforms, documents, medications, and chargers with you for the travel days.

Document electronics, furniture, and serial numbers to support any damage claims.

Verify each box and item count to ensure accuracy before the truck departs.

Clearing the Installation

Book finance, medical, and housing appointments early so you can finish all signatures.

Return issued equipment, receive clearance stamps, and keep copies of all paperwork.

Finalize flights, lodging, or POV route plans and save receipts for travel vouchers.

Arrival and Delivery

Report to your gaining command and confirm in-processing schedules as soon as you arrive.

Coordinate delivery dates and be present to inspect items at arrival.

Submit claims quickly using your move portal with photos and inventory notes.

Tip: Your progress is saved automatically on this device using local storage.
