Army Duty Station

Fort Meade, Maryland

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Meade is a major installation between Baltimore and Washington, DC that supports cyber, intelligence, and joint communications missions.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers report to the Soldier Support Center or Welcome Center for in-processing and administrative intake.

Commissary

The Fort Meade commissary provides DeCA grocery services on post.

Fort Meade Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange facilities provide retail and service options for the installation.

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • U.S. Cyber Command
  • National Security Agency/Central Security Service
  • Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA)
  • 704th Military Intelligence Brigade

Gate Information
