Army Duty Station

Fort Johnson, Louisiana

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Johnson is an Army installation in western Louisiana that hosts the Joint Readiness Training Center and supports rotational training for deploying units.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers report to the Welcome Center or Replacement Detachment for in-processing and administrative intake.

Commissary

The Fort Johnson commissary provides DeCA grocery services on post.

Fort Johnson Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange facilities provide retail and service options on the installation.

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • Joint Readiness Training Center (JRTC)
  • JRTC Operations Group
  • 1st Battalion, 509th Infantry Regiment (Airborne)
  • Fort Johnson Garrison
