Army Duty Station

Fort Stewart, Georgia

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Stewart is a large Army installation in coastal Georgia that supports mechanized infantry, aviation, and sustainment missions for the 3rd Infantry Division.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers report to the Welcome Center or Soldier Support Center for in-processing and administrative intake.

Commissary

The Fort Stewart commissary provides DeCA grocery services on post.

Fort Stewart Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange facilities provide retail and service options for the installation.

AAFES Exchange Overview

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • 3rd Infantry Division
  • 3rd Combat Aviation Brigade
  • 1st Armored Brigade Combat Team, 3rd Infantry Division
  • Division Sustainment Brigade

Gate access hours and ID requirements vary by entrance; confirm current gate status before arrival.
