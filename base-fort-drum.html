Army Duty Station

Fort Drum

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Drum is the home of the 10th Mountain Division and prepares light infantry formations for deployment to austere and cold-weather environments.

Post Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers typically report to the installation reception or welcome center for in-processing, ID card services, and administrative intake. Confirm reporting instructions in orders.

Commissary

On-post commissary services provide groceries and household essentials for eligible patrons.

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange locations provide retail, services, and dining options on the installation.

AAFES Exchange Overview

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • 10th Mountain Division
  • 1st Brigade Combat Team, 10th MTN
  • 2nd Brigade Combat Team, 10th MTN

Gate Information

Gate access hours and ID requirements vary by entrance; verify current gate status before arrival.

On-Post & DoD Housing

Official Housing Overview

Installation housing offices manage applications, waitlists, and privatized housing partners for on-post residents.

On-Post Housing Areas

Family housing neighborhoods and barracks areas are distributed across the main installation.

DoD-Managed Housing Areas

DoD-managed or privatized housing communities may be located on or adjacent to the installation.
