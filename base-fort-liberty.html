Army Duty Station

Fort Bragg

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Bragg is a major Army installation in North Carolina with a large active-duty and tenant unit presence that supports airborne, special operations, and sustainment missions.

Post Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers typically report to the installation reception and in-processing center or Reception Battalion for SRP, MPD/S1 processing, and initial administrative intake.

Commissary

North and South Post commissaries provide DeCA-operated grocery services across the installation.

Fort Bragg North Commissary Fort Bragg South Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange areas serve both North and South Post shopping and services needs.

AAFES Exchange Overview

Major Unit Areas

Major command areas include:

  • XVIII Airborne Corps
  • 82nd Airborne Division
  • U.S. Army Special Operations Command (USASOC)
  • U.S. Army Forces Command (FORSCOM)
  • U.S. Army Reserve Command (USARC)

Gate Information

Gate access hours and ID requirements vary by entrance; confirm current gate status before arrival.
