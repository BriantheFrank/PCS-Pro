Army Duty Station

Fort Bragg

North Carolina

Major units stationed here

  • XVIII Airborne Corps
  • 82nd Airborne Division
  • U.S. Army Special Operations Command

Fort Bragg is the Army's largest installation by population and a centerpiece for airborne and special operations readiness, projecting rapid-response forces from the East Coast.

Post Information

In-Processing / Reception

New arrivals typically report to the designated reception and in-processing center to begin administrative intake and unit coordination.

Commissary

Multiple commissary locations support daily grocery needs, with peak traffic around weekends and payday periods.

PX / Exchange

The main exchange area offers retail, services, and dining options central to the installation footprint.

Major Unit Areas

Concentrations include XVIII Airborne Corps, USASOC, and 82nd Airborne Division zones, so check unit maps for your assigned area.

Gate Information

Gate hours and access procedures vary by location; verify current access points before your first commute.
