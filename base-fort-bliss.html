Army Duty Station

Fort Bliss, Texas

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Bliss is a large Army installation in West Texas and southern New Mexico that supports armored, air defense, and leader development missions centered around the El Paso region.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers typically report to the Welcome Center or Replacement Detachment on main post for in-processing and administrative intake.

Commissary

The main post commissary provides DeCA grocery services for Fort Bliss households.

Fort Bliss Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange locations include Freedom Crossing and services across the installation.

AAFES Exchange Overview

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • 1st Armored Division
  • 32nd Army Air & Missile Defense Command
  • 11th Air Defense Artillery Brigade
  • U.S. Army Sergeants Major Academy

Gate Information

Gate access hours and ID requirements vary by entrance; verify current gate status before arrival.
