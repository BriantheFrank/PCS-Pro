Army Duty Station

Fort Belvoir, Virginia

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Belvoir is a major Army installation in Northern Virginia that supports headquarters, logistics, intelligence, and medical missions across the National Capital Region.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers typically report to the Soldier Support Center or Welcome Center for reception and in-processing appointments.

Commissary

The Fort Belvoir commissary provides DeCA grocery services on the installation.

Fort Belvoir Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange facilities provide retail, services, and dining options across the post.

AAFES Exchange Overview

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • Defense Logistics Agency Headquarters
  • U.S. Army Intelligence and Security Command (INSCOM)
  • U.S. Army Corps of Engineers Headquarters
  • Installation Management Command Headquarters

Gate Information

Gate access hours and ID requirements vary by entrance; confirm current gate status before arrival.
