Updating DEERS/RAPIDS Information

Accurate records keep your family covered for IDs and benefits.

← Back to PCS checklist

Why this step matters

The Defense Enrollment Eligibility Reporting System (DEERS) and the RAPIDS ID system control health coverage, ID cards, and base access. When information is current, everything else runs smoother.

Detailed sub-checklist

Why: incorrect details can delay medical care or ID cards. Check within 30 days of receiving orders.

Why: appointment wait times can be weeks. Booking early avoids last-minute rush before travel.

Why: DEERS updates often require birth certificates, marriage licenses, or adoption paperwork. Double-check requirements online before the visit.

Why: correct addresses ensure medical and benefit mail reaches you during the move. Update again once you arrive.

Why: DEERS changes affect health coverage. Confirm your plan is still active for the new location.

Common pitfalls & pro tips

  • Waiting until move week to update IDs. Appointments disappear fast.
  • Forgetting original documents. Photocopies are often not accepted.
  • If your spouse is deployed or away, ask about power of attorney requirements ahead of time.
  • Keep a folder with vital documents so you can handle updates quickly after arrival.
