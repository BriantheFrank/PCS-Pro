Army Duty Station

Fort Cavazos, Texas

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Cavazos is a large armored and sustainment installation in Central Texas supporting corps-level operations, combat brigades, and regional training missions.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers report to the replacement company or Soldier Support Center for in-processing and personnel actions.

Commissary

The main post commissary provides DeCA grocery services for Fort Cavazos families.

Fort Cavazos Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange facilities provide retail and dining services across the installation.

AAFES Exchange Overview

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • III Armored Corps
  • 1st Cavalry Division
  • 13th Expeditionary Sustainment Command
  • 3rd Cavalry Regiment

Gate Information

Gate access hours and ID requirements vary by entrance; verify current gate status before arrival.
